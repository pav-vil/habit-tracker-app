üîó Habit Stacking

Link new habits to existing routines. Example: "After I brush my teeth, I will do 5 pushups" or "After I pour my morning coffee, I will meditate for 2 minutes."

üìñ Learn More ‚Üí
üå± Start Tiny

Make it ridiculously easy to start. One pushup, one page, one minute. Once you start, you'll often do more. The goal is to show up, not to be perfect.

üìñ Learn More ‚Üí
üéØ If-Then Plans

Plan for obstacles in advance. "If I'm too tired to exercise, then I'll do just 5 minutes." "If I miss a day, then I'll get back on track tomorrow." Decide ahead, act automatically.

üìñ Learn More ‚Üí
üîÅ Consistent Repetition
